  • Title: A study on avatar self-expression in VR environment

Summary

The study investigates how self-awareness differs depending on the sort of avatar's self-expression in a virtual reality (VR) environment. Experiments with the VR social app and pre-test and post-test questionnaires were carried out to validate study topics. Consequently, most respondents decorate avatars that resemble their appearance in an actual media environment.
