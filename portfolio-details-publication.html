Thesis Summary

This study aims to elucidate the role of information and uncertainty in the relationship between VT experiences and visit intention. to clarify the above hypothesis, this study employs a mixed methodology combining VR experiments with pre-test and post-tests on 126 international students. according to the findings, information seeking is an important mediator of the path from VT to the intention to visit. the role of uncertainty as a moderator is not significant in this study, but there are signs that uncertainty will interfere with the mediation effect of information seeking. the findings will be used to discuss how to create the foundation for promoting Korean tourism through an immersive media channel in addition to the current traditional channels.

Cao Thị Bích Nga
